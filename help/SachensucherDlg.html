Sachensucher

Let's see how non-soundfiles sound.

this takes any input file and tries to spot what kind of sample data it could be (tries to find "wordlengths" and sample encoding (integer or float)). i'm not sure about the "windowlength" but i think that's the minimum length for analyzing the most probable "sample format". also not sure about the "headroom", maybe a volume factor.

sometimes nice with uncompressed images and uncompressed movies.
